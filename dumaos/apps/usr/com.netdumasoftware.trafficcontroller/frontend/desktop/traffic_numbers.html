Use this panel to see how effective your rules are. The status indicator will light up when traffic is being detected for the rule. For example, if you are blocking all traffic on a device, it will light up when DumaOS can see data is being attempted to be sent or received by the device. The packets and bytes will then increase to show the amount of data that has been blocked by your rule.
This feature can also be used for monitoring, just disable the blocking function and keep an eye on the packets.
